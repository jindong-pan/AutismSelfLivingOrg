# Non profit org for Autism and Disabled kids:
## Our mission:
Our mission is to empower children with autism through accessible therapy and family support by providing evidence-based programs and resources. We believe every child deserves the opportunity to thrive, and we strive to create inclusive communities where all children can grow.

## 我们的使命：
我们的使命是通过提供循证项目和资源，帮助自闭症儿童获得便捷的治疗和家庭支持，从而增强他们的能力。我们相信每个孩子都应该拥有茁壮成长的机会，并致力于创建包容性的社区，让所有孩子都能健康快乐地成长。
